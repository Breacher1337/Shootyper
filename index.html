Welcome to Shootyper! A game made by Elaijah.

Here are the instructions:

Type in the input box the words that appear on the screen. When you type the word correctly, the word will disappear and the chicken will shoot a fireball.

The bloons on the right will try to get the chicken, and its up to you to keep the chicken alive!

There is no end game, just try to survive as long as you can!

Did you know:

Shootyper is a combination of the words "Shooter" and "Typer"!

Inputting the word "Restart" will restart the game.

Inputting the word "Pause" will pause the game.

Press ESC to Continue.
